Welcome to Serum Blog!

Sunday, 21 May 2017 Tags: welcome

Hello, world!

This is the official blog of Serum, a static website generator written in Elixir programming language. Information about new features and significant changes will be mainly posted on this blog.

For right now, Serum is in the early development stage. Many things may change in a short period of time, and these changes may require you to modify your existing Serum projects a little bit to keep them buildable. So keep an eye on this blog to keep yourself updated!

And finally, if you have any problems, bugs or other issues, please feel free to open an issue. Your words make Serum better than yesterday!
